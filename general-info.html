Facts and History

FEU Diliman, established in 1983, was initially built for the Institute of Technology, offering upper-year engineering programs. It was the largest FEU campus in land area and later transitioned its engineering programs back to the main campus. With its growing basic education population, FEU Diliman joined the UAAP Juniors in S.Y. 2006-2007 and quickly excelled in sports, winning championships in football and chess by 2010-2011. This success led to the development of top-tier sports facilities, including one of the country’s first artificial football pitches in 2012 and a world-class Sports Complex in 2014. Today, FEU Diliman emphasizes holistic education, character formation, and innovative learning experiences to prepare students for the future and is now on their 12 consecutive championship in the high school football tournament in the 2024 season.
